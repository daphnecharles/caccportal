ResNet Ticket Procedures (for Sups)

Equipment Sign-Out

  1. Log onto HDRT and open up ticket# 387407 (or click on the Equipment Signout link on left)
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the ticket and copy the information from the last comment
  3. With the copied information, paste it into a new comment and update each item's status. If any equipment is going out, note who it's with and what ticket# it's for
  4. The ticket's status is Open whenever ANY item is out, and Resolved when everything is back in the TD room. You can leave the owner of the ticket as it is
  5. Return items to TD room closet, top shelf

 

Minihubs

Releasing a Hub
  1. Grab a minihub, power supply, Ethernet cable, and minihub release form from the cabinet in the TD room
  2. Login to HDRT and go to the "Dorms - minihubs" queue. Locate the specific minihub by its identifier found on the hub (ex. CAC-MH-001) and open the ticket (or click on the corresponding minihub ticket link on left)
  3. Click on the "Special Fields" link on the ticket page and update all the information, which Dispatch should have for you. This automatically updates the ticket to Open
  4. Remind Dispatch to have the user fill out the minihub form and to bring back the top-half portion, which should be filed into the folder located on the TD cabinet
Returning a Hub
  1. Dispatch will receive a ticket to retrieve them. Get a return form for Dispatch from the TD room for these visits. Remind them to bring back the top-half portion
  2. When Dispatch returns, collect all items they have brought back and put it in the TD room. File the return form in the folder on the cabinet and note the minihub's identifier (ex. CAC-MH-001)
  3. Find the minihub's ticket in "Dorms - minihubs" queue and open the ticket (or click on the corresponding minihub ticket link on left)
  4. Click on the "Special Fields" link on the ticket page and clear out the fields
  5. Click on the "Basics" link at the top of the page and erase the building/room# from the subject, leaving only the identifier
  6. Comment to say who returned it and set the status of the ticket to Stalled
